VISAKHAPATNAM

Overview

The ancient city of Visakhapatnam or Vizag is located in the Indian state of Andhra Pradesh. It is a city charting its course by navigating the waves of traditional culture with a fresh breeze of cosmopolitanism. Visakhapatnam boasts several ancient Buddhist establishments such as Sankaram, Bojjannakonda, Bavikonda, Totlakonda, Boudharamam, Saligudam, and Devipuram which are at least two millennia old. It is blessed with pristine beaches such as Lawson's Bay, Rushikonda, Bheemili and Gangavaram beaches ideal for sea bathing and surfing. One can unwind at Keertana Vanamu Beach Resort, while Bheemunipatnam is a beach town ideal for spending weekends.


POPULAR PLACES

SIMHACHALAM TEMPLE

Simhachalam Temple is also known as Simhadri temple. The temple derives its name from two Sanskrit words, “Simha” meaning lion and “Achala” meaning hill. Therefore, built on the top of the hill, the temple is dedicated to incarnation of Lord Vishnu and is called Simhachalam. The folk believe that Lord Narasimha saved child Prahlada from evil tortures of his father, Hiranyakasipu. Simhachalam Temple is among the eighteen shrines of Lord Narasimha in India.

BORRA CAVES

Borra Caves lie on the eastern coast of country, in Ananthagiri hills of Araku Valley. These caves were discovered in 1807 by William King, an officer of Geological Survey of India. The legend says that a cow grazing on the meadow fell into the deep cave. On searching people found it inside the cave and also found a Lingam there. The folk believed that Lord Shiva protected the cow and constructed the temple outside the cave. Large numbers of visitors reach the temple to worship Lord Shiva. They can also explore the Lingam and other nearby sites in the caves. Borra caves are the largest caves in the country.

KATIKI FALLS

Katiki Waterfalls come on the way to the famous Borra Caves. It is just 7 kilometres away from Borra Caves. These waterfalls are among the renowned tourists sites in Vishakhapatnam. The waterfalls are named after a small nearby village named Katiki. You will hear the roaring sound of water standing at the bottom of the waterfalls. The scenic beauty of the Katiki waterfalls is mesmerizingly beautiful. The tourists open spend their leisure time in the lap of nature.

BHEEMUNIPATNAM BEACH

The Bheemunipatnam Beach is located about 24 kilometres away from Vishakhapatnam. The river Gosthani flow near the beach and has crystal clear turquoise water. The beach is famous for its red sand that is found along the banks of the river. The Bheemunipatnam Beach got its name from mythological epic. History reveals that Bheema killed a demon named Bakasura at the site where the present day beach is located. From the day, the beach is known as Bheemunipatnam or the Bheemli beach.

Languages Spoken

Telugu is the official language but two dialects of Telugu are spoken by the people. English and Hindi are also widely spoken.

Best Season to Visit

From October to March, the weather loses its stinging heat and becomes bearable with temperatures ranging between 18°C to 32°C. The months between November and January see many festivals and fairs organised such as Diwali festival in November, Christmas in December and in January, the three-day annual Visakha Utsav festival is held highlighting Andhra culture.

Shopping

Lepakshi Handicrafts- An ideal destination for handicrafts shopping, this place is handled by the Andhra Government and is the main centre for handicraft goods in Vizag. Daba Gardens offers some great sports goods. All the sports items you will find here are of good quality and are priced very reasonably. Kurupam Market is a great place to buy gold and silver jewellery. The market is located by the Hindu Reading Room and is considered one of the best places to shop in Vizag.

How to reach


BY FLIGHT

Visakhapatnam Airport is connected by daily flights to Chennai, Delhi, Mumbai, Bangalore, Hyderabad, Kolkata, Kochi, Mangalore, Bhubaneswar, Raipur, Tirupathi, Port Blair and other towns and cities. IndiGo, SpiceJet, Air India, Air Costa, Jet Airways, AirAsia,Alliance Air, Singapore Airlines, Silkair etc. are operational from here and international flights to Dubai, Singapore and Kuala Lumpur have been initiated.

BY RAIL

Visakhapatnam railway station is located on the Chennai Central-Howrah Station route, connecting it to many Indian towns and cities such as Hyderabad, Delhi, Kolkata, Chennai, Bangalore, Mumbai and the like. Chennai Express, Guwahati Express, Konark Express, Godavari Express, Howrah Mail, etc. are operational here..

BY ROAD

Visakhapatnam is connected by NH-5, a major highway and part of the Golden Quadrilateral network of Indian highways connecting Chennai and Kolkata. State-owned APSRTC buses reach various places like Kakinada, Rajahmundry, Guntur, Kadapa, Vijayawada, Kurnool, Ongole, Chennai, Bangalore, Kolkata, Bhubaneswar etc. The state government offers interstate bus services to states like and Tamil Nadu, Andhra Pradesh and Karnataka.
